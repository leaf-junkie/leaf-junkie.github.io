Hi, I'm Julie!


Thanks for taking a moment to visit my page. I am currently a student in a full stack web development bootcamp. While I can work a full stack (MERN), I would consider myself T-shaped and much prefer the front end. As an artist, I am highly passionate about design and believe I would thrive working in UX and/or UI. I am eager to continue learning about the ways that users interact with the elements of a website or application. Additionally, I feel strongly about the significant contributions that subtle details add to a website.


I primarily work with JavaScript (and, of course, HTML and CSS). Check out my resume to see more of the technologies I know. I have stumbled upon the vast community dedicated to CSS animations and am fascinated by it! I often find myself down the rabbit hole exploring CSS frameworks and JavaScript animation libraries. And don't get me started on typography! Anyway, what better way to learn more about me as a developer than to check out my portfolio?
